# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 14, 2025

## Introduction

Vibe University ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our educational platform.

## FERPA Compliance

We comply with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA), 20 U.S.C. ยง 1232g, which protects the privacy of student education records. We recognize that students and parents have certain rights under FERPA regarding educational records.

### Your Rights Under FERPA

1. **Right to Inspect and Review**: You have the right to inspect and review your educational records.
2. **Right to Request Amendment**: You have the right to request amendment of educational records that you believe are inaccurate or misleading.
3. **Right to Consent to Disclosure**: You have the right to consent to disclosures of personally identifiable information contained in your educational records, except to the extent that FERPA authorizes disclosure without consent.
4. **Right to File a Complaint**: You have the right to file a complaint with the U.S. Department of Education concerning alleged failures by the institution to comply with FERPA.

## Information We Collect

- **Account Information**: Email address, name, educational institution

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. Provide and maintain our educational services
2. Personalize your learning experience
3. Improve our platform and develop new features
4. Communicate with you about your account and updates
5. Ensure academic integrity and prevent misuse

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

- **Encryption at Rest**: All sensitive data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
- **Encryption in Transit**: All data transmitted is protected using HTTPS/TLS
- **Access Controls**: Strict access controls and authentication requirements
- **Regular Audits**: Security audits and vulnerability assessments
- **Audit Logging**: Comprehensive logging of all data access

## Data Retention

We retain your information as follows:

- **Active Educational Records**: 7 years from creation or last modification
- **Audit Logs**: 5 years from creation
- **Deleted Account Data**: 90 days after deletion request

## Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

1. **Access Your Data**: Request a copy of your personal information
2. **Correct Your Data**: Request correction of inaccurate information
3. **Delete Your Data**: Request deletion of your personal information
4. **Export Your Data**: Receive your data in a portable format
5. **Withdraw Consent**: Withdraw your consent to data processing

## State-Specific Privacy Rights

### California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
- Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

### European Residents (GDPR)

EU residents have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

- Right to access
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
