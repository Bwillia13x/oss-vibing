## Quick Start Guides

### For Students

#### Getting Started (5 minutes)

**Creating Your First Document:**

1. Log in with your university email
2. Click "New Document" button
3. Enter a title (e.g., "My Research Paper")
4. Start writing in the editor
5. Your work auto-saves every few seconds âœ…

**Adding Citations:**

1. Place cursor where you want the citation
2. Click "Add Citation" or press `Ctrl+K`
3. Search by DOI, title, or author
4. Select the paper from results
5. Citation automatically inserted and formatted!

**Exporting Your Work:**

1. Click "Export" in the toolbar
2. Choose format (PDF, DOCX, etc.)
3. Select citation style (APA, MLA, Chicago)
4. Download your formatted document

### For Instructors

#### Setting Up Your Class (10 minutes)

**Creating an Assignment:**

1. Navigate to "Assignments" tab
2. Click "New Assignment"
3. Fill in details:
   - Title: "Climate Change Essay"
   - Due date: Select from calendar
   - Points: 100
   - Requirements: Min 10 citations, 2000 words
4. Click "Publish to Class"

**Viewing Student Submissions:**

1. Go to assignment page
2. See submission status for each student
3. Click student name to view their work
4. See real-time writing progress (if enabled)

**Grading Workflow:**

1. Open student submission
2. Review content and citations
3. Check plagiarism report (if enabled)
4. Enter grade and feedback:
   - Overall comments
   - Rubric scores (if applicable)
   - Inline comments on specific sections
5. Click "Submit Grade"
6. Grade syncs to your LMS automatically

- `Ctrl+S` - Manual save
- `Ctrl+K` - Add citation
- `Ctrl+B` - Bold text

**Citation Search Tips:**
- Use DOI for fastest results (e.g., `10.1234/example`)
- Search by author: `author:"Smith"`
- Search by year: `year:2023`
- Search by title: Partial matches work

## FAQ

### Students

**Q: How do I recover a deleted document?**
A: Deleted documents are moved to trash for 30 days. Click "Trash" to restore.

**Q: Can I work offline?**
A: Yes! Changes sync when you reconnect to internet.

**Q: How many citations can I add?**
A: Unlimited! The system handles hundreds of citations per document.

**Q: Can I export to Google Docs?**
A: Yes, export as DOCX and upload to Google Drive.

**Q: Is my work private?**
A: Yes, documents are private by default. You control sharing.

### Instructors

**Q: Can I reuse assignments from previous semesters?**
A: Yes! Use "Duplicate Assignment" to reuse with new due dates.

**Q: How do I bulk grade submissions?**
A: Use the grading interface to quickly move between submissions.

**Q: Can I import grades from another system?**
A: Yes, via CSV upload or LMS sync.

**Q: What citation formats are supported?**
A: APA, MLA, Chicago, IEEE, and more. Custom styles available.

**Q: Can TAs help with grading?**
A: Yes, assign TA role and delegate grading tasks.

### Administrators

**Q: How do I add more seats to our license?**
A: Contact sales or upgrade via admin panel.

**Q: What happens when a license expires?**
A: Users retain read-only access. Renewals restore full access.

**Q: How is data backed up?**
A: Automated daily backups with 30-day retention. Geographic redundancy enabled.

**Q: Can we use our own SSO?**
A: Yes, SAML/OAuth integrations supported.

**Q: How do I export all user data (FERPA request)?**
A: Use admin API: `GET /api/admin/users/{userId}/export`
