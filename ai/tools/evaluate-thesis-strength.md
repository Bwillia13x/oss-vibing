Evaluate the strength and quality of a thesis statement in an academic document.

This tool provides detailed analysis of thesis statements to help students craft stronger, more effective arguments. It evaluates:
- Clarity and specificity
- Argumentative nature (debatable vs. descriptive)
- Scope appropriateness (too broad or too narrow)
- Discipline-specific conventions
- Originality and contribution
- Testability and falsifiability (for empirical work)

Use this tool when:
- Student is drafting or revising a thesis
- Need to assess if thesis meets assignment requirements
- Want to compare thesis strength across drafts
- Preparing for thesis defense or proposal

The tool provides actionable recommendations for improvement and can compare the thesis against best practices for specific academic disciplines.
