Plan and organize semester schedule by merging courses, tasks, and exams into a unified calendar.

This tool creates semester plans:
- Imports ICS calendar feeds from LMS (Canvas, Blackboard, etc.)
- Merges course schedules with assignment deadlines
- Identifies scheduling conflicts
- Proposes weekly study plans
- Creates task prioritization based on due dates
- Saves to courses/ and tasks/ folders

Helps students manage their academic workload effectively.
