Generate flashcards from notes for spaced repetition study using SM-2 algorithm.

This tool creates study materials from academic notes:
- Generates flashcards in cloze, Q&A, or term/definition format
- Implements SM-2 spaced repetition scheduling
- Tracks ease factor, interval, and due dates
- Saves to notes/ or study/ folder with metadata

Students can use these for efficient memorization and exam preparation.
