Review flashcards using the SM-2 spaced repetition algorithm for optimal retention.

This tool manages flashcard review sessions:
- Loads flashcard decks and identifies cards due for review
- Implements SM-2 algorithm for optimal spacing
- Updates ease factors and intervals based on recall quality
- Tracks progress and retention statistics
- Schedules next review sessions automatically

Students should rate their recall quality (0-5) to optimize learning intervals.
