Automatically synthesize and organize literature review sections from research papers.

This tool helps generate comprehensive, well-structured literature review sections by analyzing and organizing research papers. It:
- Groups papers by themes, topics, and research questions
- Identifies key authors and seminal works
- Synthesizes findings across multiple studies
- Highlights agreements and contradictions
- Generates narrative summaries of literature
- Creates thematic organization for review sections
- Suggests gaps and future directions

Use this tool when:
- Writing literature review chapters or sections
- Organizing large collections of papers
- Synthesizing findings across multiple studies
- Creating thematic structures for reviews
- Need to understand consensus and debates in field
- Building background sections for research papers

The tool analyzes your reference collection and generates organized, synthesis-focused literature review text that integrates findings across sources rather than just listing papers.
