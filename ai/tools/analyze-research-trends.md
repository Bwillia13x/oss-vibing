Analyze research trends and topic evolution over time in academic literature.

This tool tracks how research topics, methods, and themes evolve across publications over time. It identifies:
- Emerging topics and increasing research interest
- Declining topics and waning interest areas
- Shifting methodologies and approaches
- Temporal patterns in publication frequency
- Hot topics and research momentum
- Historical development of research areas

Use this tool when:
- Identifying current hot topics in a field
- Understanding historical context of research areas
- Finding emerging opportunities or declining fields
- Planning research direction based on trends
- Writing literature review sections on field development
- Justifying research timeliness and relevance

The tool analyzes references over time to reveal temporal patterns and trends in research focus, methodology, and activity levels.
